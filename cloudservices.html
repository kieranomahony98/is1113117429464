During the past two years Netflix has pulled back the curtains to provide a behind-the-scenes glimpse into how it runs one of the most popular video
streaming websites on the Internet, almost entirely in the public cloud. The company has open sourced dozens of tools it’s
developed internally. In doing so, some argue that Netflix is turning into one of the most important cloud computing companies
in the industry, by sharing with developers how it’s being done and providing others with a path to follow.
